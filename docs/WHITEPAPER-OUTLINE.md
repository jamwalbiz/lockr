# Whitepaper outline

## Prediction Markets vs Sportsbooks: The 2026 State of the Bettor

### 150-word intro

The line between betting a game and trading a contract has blurred. In 2026 a sports fan can take a side on a sportsbook, or buy a yes or no contract on the same outcome at a CFTC-regulated venue like Kalshi, or a crypto-native one like Polymarket. The mechanics, the fees, the regulation, and the tax treatment are all different, and most bettors do not know which one actually serves them better on a given play. This paper lays out the honest tradeoffs. It is written for the person putting real money down, not for a regulator or a press release. We compare pricing and vig, liquidity, regulation and custody, the breadth of markets, and where each option is even legal. We do not promise a winning system. We do show where a disciplined bettor finds a real edge, and where the hype outruns the math. Bet only what you can afford to lose.
