# Lockr — PRODUCT.md

## Register
**brand** — the marketing site (joinlockr.com) IS the product surface. Design communicates, it doesn't transact (checkout is handed to Whop's embed).

## What it is
Lockr is the intelligence brand for **sports betting + prediction markets**. The flagship product is a paid membership delivering daily plays (sports + Kalshi/Polymarket) with reasoning, recommended sizing, and a public, permanent record of every result. The brand is built to expand beyond picks into content, education (e-books, courses), affiliate, and sponsorships — a media/intelligence business, not a "picks Discord."

## Target users
- **Core:** US sports bettors, casual-to-serious, who are tired of touts who fake or delete their losses.
- **Expansion:** prediction-market participants (Kalshi/Polymarket), and complete beginners who want to be walked through what to bet, how much, and why.
- Language must be understandable by **all ages and expertise** — a first-time bettor and a seven-day-a-week grinder both get it.

## Brand personality
Serious, precise, transparent, unsentimental. A precision instrument, not a hype machine. The opposite of gold-Lambo tout culture. Confident but welcoming — never condescending to beginners.

## The wedge (core differentiator)
**Transparency.** Most touts (e.g. TopPick/Umar's model) delete the group daily so the losing record vanishes. Lockr logs every play in public — win or loss, timestamped before the event, never deleted. Plus prediction markets, which most picks services don't touch.

## Founder
Jairo Tovar ("JT") is the founder and credibility anchor (About page). The **brand voice is Lockr-first** — you buy Lockr, not "JT's Discord" (model: Whoop, Action Network). JT stays visible as founder; the business is built to stand on its own (sellable, sponsorable, expandable).

## Anti-references (what Lockr is NOT)
- Gaudy tout aesthetic: gold Lambos, "MAX BET / SUPERMAX BET", "$400-600k play", cash-and-chains. Irresponsible-sizing culture.
- Deleted-Telegram / no-track-record model.
- Generic centered-SaaS-hero "picks Discord" landing page.

## Strategic design principles
1. **Transparency made visual** — receipts, timestamps, "logged in public" are the hero, not flexed winnings.
2. **Premium-technical, not hype** — dark, terminal-adjacent, mono data, restrained neon-green accent.
3. **Welcoming precision** — sophisticated look, plain language. No jargon walls.
4. **Same-day value** — "join today, bet tonight" is a real hook; surface it.
5. **Elastic IA** — leave room for free content, blog, courses, tools; don't hard-code "Discord = the product."

## Visual system (committed identity — preserve)
- bg `#0a0a0b`, surface `#141416`, ink `#f5f4f1`, accent `#00ff85` (neon green), gold `#c9a76a` (Inner Circle), danger `#ff4d4d`.
- Display: **Archivo** (technical grotesque). Body: **Inter**. Data/numerics: **JetBrains Mono**.
- Motifs: chart-grid background, radial green glow, the `[•] LOCKR` inline mark, terminal-panel UI language.
