THE FILM. A ~40s brand film for Lockr. 16:9, 1920x1080, 30fps. Register: tier-1 agency, hyperkinetic, Buck Studio x Sucuk energy. Smash cuts and peak-action frames that punch. Expensive, controlled, type-forward. Silent and sound-design-ready, no voiceover.

LOCK THESE, everything else is yours. Palette: near-black void #0a0a0b, warm off-white text #f5f4f1 (never pure white), one bright accent, neon green #00ff85, used sparingly like it is precious.
